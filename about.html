Our History

Established in New York City over fifteen years ago, SneakersParadise revolutionized sneaker retail as the original consignment store for rare shoes. Carrying the rarest exclusives and collectible sneakers, SneakersParadise has evolved from a one-stop sneaker destination, to a cultural hub for sneaker enthusiasts and novices alike.With three brick-and-mortar locations in New York City, Los Angeles and Miami, SneakersParadise remains the premier source for authentic, rare sneakers.
